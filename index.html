Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains pipe roundness. Our PE100 and PE80 grade raw materials from certified suppliers ensure consistent molecular weight distribution and optimal melt flow index for superior pipe performance.

Precision Extrusion Process

Our advanced twin-screw extruders provide uniform melt temperature and pressure, ensuring consistent wall thickness and dimensional stability throughout the entire pipe length.

Controlled Cooling System

Gradual, controlled cooling in multiple water baths prevents residual stress buildup and ensures dimensional stability, determining the final mechanical properties of the pipe.

Precision Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains pipe roundness. Laser measurement systems provide real-time dimensional feedback.

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes comprehensive testing including hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional verification, visual inspection, and material property analysis.

Permanent Marking & Traceability

Each pipe is permanently marked with product specifications, batch number, certification marks, and manufacturing date for complete traceability.

Precision Cutting to Length

Automated cutting systems produce clean, square pipe ends at exact specified lengths. Burr-free cutting ensures proper joint preparation and optimal fusion welding performance.

Secure Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are carefully bundled, capped, and secured for safe transportation. Our packaging protects pipe ends and surfaces during handling, storage, and transit.
